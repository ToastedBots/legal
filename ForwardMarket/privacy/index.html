This Privacy Policy applies to ForwardMarket, a Discord bot that provides informational Forward Assault market tools.

1. Data we collect and store

ForwardMarket does not intentionally collect personal data for normal market lookup commands. When users configure alert features, the bot may store limited operational data needed to deliver those alerts:

  • Discord user ID.
  • Discord guild ID.
  • Discord channel ID for channel alerts.
  • Optional Discord role ID for alert pings.
  • Alert filters, such as item type, weapon, skin or camo, rarity, condition, and price thresholds.
  • Alert timestamps, such as creation time and last triggered time.

2. Message content

No Message Content storage: ForwardMarket is built around slash commands, buttons, select menus, and autocomplete. It does not use Discord Message Content access and does not store user message text for normal bot use.

Command options and interaction choices may be processed temporarily to respond to a command or create an alert, but ForwardMarket does not keep general chat logs.

3. Forward Assault market data

ForwardMarket uses Forward Assault market offer and listing data to provide prices, charts, listings, history, and alerts. This market data may include seller IDs, seller names, prices, item metadata, listing dates, removal dates, and historical listing records.

Market data is separate from Discord account data. It is used to power the bot's informational market features.

4. How we use data

Data is used to operate ForwardMarket features, including market lookups, price history, charts, listings, price alerts, channel alerts, role pings, autocomplete, and owner-only maintenance views.

Data is not sold, used for advertising, or used to train machine learning or AI models.

5. Data retention

Alert configuration data may be kept until the alert is removed, disabled, or no longer needed for bot operation. Market listing and history data may be stored longer than 30 days where needed for price history, charts, trend analysis, alert matching, and service maintenance.

6. Deletion requests

Alert removal: Users can remove their own alerts through bot commands such as /alert remove.

For broader privacy questions or requests related to stored Discord alert data, contact the official support or community contact path for the bot. Approved requests are reviewed and processed against the bot's stored operational data where applicable.

7. Security

ForwardMarket stores operational data on the bot operator's infrastructure. Access to stored data is limited to authorized operators who need it for maintenance, support, security, or compliance purposes.

8. Discord and GitHub

ForwardMarket operates on Discord and uses Discord API data to provide bot features. This legal site is hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub may process visitor information for security and service operation when this site is visited.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated when ForwardMarket features, data handling, or compliance requirements change. The latest version will be available at this URL.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, use the official ForwardMarket support or community contact path.
